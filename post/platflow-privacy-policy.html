Platflow Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Platflow ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension for AI-powered web automation.

Information We Collect

1. Authentication Information

  • Email Address: Collected through Auth0 when you create an account or log in
  • Authentication Tokens: Stored locally to maintain your login session

2. Workflow Data

  • Workflow Configurations: Your custom workflow settings and preferences
  • Execution Logs: Temporary logs of workflow execution for debugging (not permanently stored)

3. Browser Storage

  • Local Storage: User preferences and settings stored in your browser
  • Session Data: Temporary data during active workflow execution

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Browsing history outside of workflow execution
  • Personal content from websites (only processed temporarily during workflows)
  • Financial or payment information
  • Health records or medical data (even if processed by workflows)
  • Location data
  • Personal communications (emails, messages, etc.)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Authenticate and identify users
  • Save and manage your custom workflows
  • Execute automation tasks as requested
  • Improve the extension's functionality
  • Provide technical support when needed

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Most data is stored locally in your browser
  • Backend Connection: The extension connects to a locally-hosted backend (via Docker) for AI processing
  • Encryption: Authentication tokens are securely stored
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store your personal data on external servers

Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for purposes unrelated to the extension's functionality
  • Transfer your data for creditworthiness or lending purposes

We only share data with:

  • Auth0: For secure authentication services
  • OpenAI API: Workflow instructions are sent to OpenAI for AI processing (no personal data included)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your workflow configurations
  • Opt-out of data collection by uninstalling the extension

Data Retention

  • Authentication data is retained while your account is active
  • Workflow configurations are stored until you delete them
  • Temporary execution data is cleared after each workflow completes

Children's Privacy

Platflow is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
